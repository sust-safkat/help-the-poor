Workshop on LaTeX

by Start Bootstrap

A workshop on LaTeX was hosted by IEEE SUST SB on 12th April, 2017. Jibesh Kanti Saha, the honorable teacher of EEE department was present as a guest on that workshop. He gave a valuable speech based on the importance of LaTeX to make our documents gentle and announce the opening of the event. The main speakers of this event were Hriteswar Talukdar, Md. Mahfuzul Azam Ghuri and Tahmid Aziz Chowdhury, all of them are associated with IEEE Organization. Various types of formatting , writing styles, citation techniques, inserting table , pictures and difficult lengthy equation techniques via LaTeX were widely described on that workshop. As it is an interesting topic , lot of interested students attend the workshop. The amount of participants were 63 and students from different departments attended the workshop. They asked a lot of relevant questions and the exponents console them with feasible answers. The workshop lasted for 2 hours and at the end the SB Chair gave his ending speech and announce the end of the event. The viewers enjoyed the workshop so much that, they told to organize another workshop on LaTeX to know more about text formatting.
